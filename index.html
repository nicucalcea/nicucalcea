Other media roles, London/Chișinău
JUL 2010 - APR 2020
I started my first paid journalism job in 2010, at age 16, when I co-founded Privesc.Eu, a Moldovan media startup that broadcasted live from press conferences and other public interest events. After I graduated from City University, I started my first data job at dataviz agency infogr8, where I helped companies like Spotify, Billboard, Oracle and Mars present their data in the best way possible.

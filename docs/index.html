 BBC • November 2023

Energy prices: How much will you pay from January?

The typical annual energy bill for a household in England, Wales and Scotland has increased to £1,928 per year for the first three months of 2024, a rise of £94 from November-December 2023. The figure is based on how much energy the typical household uses. However, how much you'll actually end up paying depends on how much you use, where you live, and how you pay.

Also covered by: Yahoo News

BBC • December 2022

Cost of living: If no one comes, we close early, says pub

Business reporters, BBC NewsAt the Barn Pub near Rugby, the drinks are ready to be served, but unless lots of customers arrive, staff leave early. "If no regulars come in, we close early," the chef says. "It costs to keep this building heated."The Barn Pub is not alone. A survey from the British Beer and Pub Association, seen by the BBC, suggested most pubs are thinking of cutting down trading hours over the winter.
